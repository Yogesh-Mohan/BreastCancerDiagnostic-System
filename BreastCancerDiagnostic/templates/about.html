A technical overview of the Wisconsin Breast Cancer Dataset and the machine learning model validation structures.

Dataset Cytological Feature Definitions

This system uses the Wisconsin Breast Cancer FNA database. Cell nuclei parameters are computed from digitized images of a fine needle aspirate (FNA) of a breast mass. They describe characteristics of the cell nuclei present in the sample.

Nucleus Feature Clinical Description
Radius Mean Mean of distances from center to points on the outer boundary of the nucleus.
Texture Mean Standard deviation of gray-scale intensity values in the nucleus area (measures surface roughness).
Perimeter Mean Total boundary circumference length of the cell nucleus.
Area Mean Total two-dimensional surface area occupied by the cell nucleus.
Smoothness Mean Represents the local variation in nucleus radius lengths.
Compactness Mean Calculated as $Perimeter^2 / Area - 1.0$, measures the circular complexity.
Concavity Mean Severity of concave segments or indentations of the nucleus boundary.
Concave Points Mean Number of concave portions along the nucleus contour line.
Symmetry Mean Symmetry coefficient comparing nuclear hemispheres.
Fractal Dimension Mean Measures boundary complexity using coastline approximation ($coastline - 1.0$).
